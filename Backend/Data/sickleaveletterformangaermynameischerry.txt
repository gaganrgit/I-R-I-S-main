Subject: Sick Leave Request

Dear [Manager's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I am unable to come to work today due to illness. I woke up this morning feeling unwell and decided it would be best for me to stay home and rest.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to make up for any missed work once I am feeling better. I will keep you updated on my progress and let you know as soon as I am able to return to the office.

In the meantime, please let me know if there is anything I can do to help from home or if there are any specific tasks that need to be completed in my absence.

Thank you for your understanding.
